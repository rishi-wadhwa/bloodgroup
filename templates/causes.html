About us

My Blood Bank (MBB) provide you the access to any blood group required by the patients suffering from some disease or some accident. You can donate your blood to help the needies to recover soon simply by signing up and filling your details and your contact information. You can also get specific blood group if in stock.

We aim at maintaining all the information pertaining to blood donors, different blood groups available in each blood bank and help them manage in a better way.
